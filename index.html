About Me

I am a recent Computer Science graduate from Brac University, equipped with a robust foundation in problem-solving, machine learning, cybersecurity, and software engineering. My academic journey has been marked by hands-on experience in developing secure web applications and crafting efficient data pipelines for large datasets, such as my thesis project on 4D fMRI analysis using 3D CNN architectures for neurodevelopmental disorder detection.
