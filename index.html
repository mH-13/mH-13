About Me

I am a recent Computer Science graduate from Brac University with a strong foundation in problem solving, machine learning, cybersecurity, and software engineering. I have built secure web applications and designed data pipelines for large datasets – including a 4D fMRI analysis pipeline as part of my thesis on 3D CNN architectures for neurodevelopmental disorder detection.

My technical expertise spans Python, C++, JavaScript, and SQL, and I have hands-on experience in DevOps, Linux systems, and cloud-based deployments. As a passionate educator and team player, I have mentored students in computer science fundamentals while leading workshops on algorithms and data structures.

Constantly learning and innovating, I strive to create scalable, clean, and maintainable solutions that drive real-world impact. I am dedicated to merging advanced research with practical applications – a vision that guides my work and future endeavors.
